Features of our Work

Rapid development of performing and responsive applications

  • Cross Browser Support

    Each app we build is tested in major desktop, tablet, and mobile browsers and fully featured in Firefox, Chrome, Safari, Opera, and Internet Explorer. Includes support for Internet Explorer 8 & 9 via a fallback theme.

  • Customizable Options + API

    A custom project is just that - limitless options for complete control over how your application behaves. Customizable features include cloud services, touch support, social media integration, custom events, callbacks, and more. All carefully documented for ease of use.

  • Responsive & Smooth

    Use media queries and your favourite application features for desktop, tablet, mobile, and other devices. Includes the benefit of hardware acceleration and the potential to reach a silky smooth 60 frames per second, even on mobile devices.

  • Off the Shelf Themes

    Quickly add one of our ready-made themes to your web page and easily modify using just HTML and CSS. Visit the Theme Store for a growing collection of stylish WebGuru themes.

  • Rapid Animations

    Our staff specialize in expert animations and interactivity for your app. Visit our work page where we showcase some of our award winning work.

  • Ongoing Tech Support

    Support for your business doesn't end after the project. We have staff available on an ongoing basis to help with follow up questions and expertise.
